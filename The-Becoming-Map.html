The Becoming Map

A personal patterns assessment for women in the identity shift of midlife. This is where we begin to understand how your mind is moving through this season — so the coaching meets you exactly where you are.

There are no right answers here. These questions are designed to reveal the patterns that have been quietly running your inner world — the ones shaping how you see yourself, how you make decisions, and how much you trust what you find inside.

Read each question slowly. Choose the response that feels most true right now — not the one you wish were true. This usually takes around 15–20 minutes.
